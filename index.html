About Friendship Day

Friendship Day is celebrated on the first Sunday of August every year. It's a day to honor and appreciate the people who bring joy and support to our lives. The day is dedicated to celebrating the essence of friendship, and people often exchange gifts and spend time with their friends to strengthen their bonds.
